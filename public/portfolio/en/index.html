INTERACTIVE SOLAR SIMULATION
Application developed in Three.js for real-time sun position visualization. This tool allows analysis of solar incidence throughout the day and year, being useful for solar orientation studies in architectural projects. You can adjust the date, time and location to observe how the sun's position affects natural lighting.
